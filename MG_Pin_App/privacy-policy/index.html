Privacy Policy for MG Pin App

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

MG Pin App is a private automation tool used to create Pinterest Pins for my own Etsy product listings on my own Pinterest account.

The app is not a public service. It does not allow other users to create accounts, upload content, or connect their Pinterest accounts.

Information Used by the App

MG Pin App uses product information provided by the account owner, including Etsy listing URLs, product titles, product descriptions, and product images.

This information is used only to create Pinterest Pins that link to the corresponding Etsy listings.

Pinterest API Access

MG Pin App uses Pinterest API access only after authorization through Pinterest OAuth. The app may access the authorized Pinterest account’s boards and create Pins on that account.

The app does not post on behalf of other Pinterest users.

OAuth Tokens

OAuth tokens may be stored locally on the account owner’s computer to allow the app to create Pins on the authorized Pinterest account.

These tokens are not sold, shared, transferred, or made available to third parties.

Personal Data

MG Pin App does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information from Pinterest users or visitors.

The app is used only by the account owner for managing and publishing the account owner’s own Pinterest content.

Third-Party Services

The app may interact with Pinterest through the Pinterest API and may create Pins that link to Etsy listing pages. Pinterest and Etsy each have their own privacy policies and terms.

Data Sharing

MG Pin App does not sell or share personal data with third parties.

Contact
